# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Primary users are Candidates looking to verify their technical skills and build proof-of-ability portfolios, and Recruiters looking to hire vetted talent through zero-bias pipelines and adjust upstream syllabi to match their hiring requirements.

## Product Purpose
CareerOS is a unified workspace that bridges candidate learning academy tracks with real-time recruiter pipelines. It offers gamified skill validation assessments and allows recruiters to view verified skill trees of candidates and request custom course adjustments.

## Positioning
CareerOS connects physical talent supply vectors directly with market demand patterns through consensus-driven verification engines.

## Brand Personality
Clean, verified, professional, and high-contrast. The tone is authoritative and developer-centric, avoiding generic visual templates.

## Anti-references
- No generic purple-to-indigo gradients.
- No side-accent card borders (e.g. `border-l-2`).
- No bouncy or heavy web animations (e.g. `animate-bounce`).
- No low-contrast text on colored panels.

## Design Principles
- **Verified Legitimacy**: Visual layouts must reflect the precision and trustworthiness of cryptographic skill proofs.
- **Accented Focus**: UI states use solid, deliberate indigo and emerald highlights rather than decorative gradients.
- **Information Density**: Content is organized in clear grid cards that support scanning and quick data parsing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio minimum of 4.5:1 for all readable text (WCAG AA).
- Support for reduced motion (disabling decorative pulse or bounce animations).
